Commune with the Dead

A GDEX 2020 Game Jam Submission

Description

In this game, the player traverses though a haunted house with the goal of befriending all of the ghosts wandering the house. After they do that they can go to their burial and let the ghosts be free!

This game was a colaberation effort between myself and 2 others for the GDEX 2020 Game Jam

Controls

  • Jump - Space
  • Interact - F
  • Double Jump (Yellow Ghost) - Space (airborne)
  • Fight (Red Ghost) - E

Credits

  • Art: Kayleigh Jones
  • Programming (Payer): Jacob Bridenthal
  • Programming (Ghosts & Level Building): Patrick Tener
