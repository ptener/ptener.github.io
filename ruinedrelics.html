Ruined Relics

Create wacky sculptures with an array of bones and objects

Ruined Relics's Development

Explore an excavation site to find bones and "relics" to build a museum exhibit. The pieces may or may not reflect accurate natural history.

This game was a colaberation effort between myself and 5 others for the 2021 Global Game Jam.

Controls

    First Person View
  • Move - WSAD/Arrow Keys
  • Interact - E
  • Place Bone - B
  • Pause - Escape
    Display View
  • Pan Camera - WSAD/Arrow Keys
  • Swap Cammera - E
  • Pause - Escape

I am in the progress of implementing all the assest that were created for this project into the game so it can feel complete.
