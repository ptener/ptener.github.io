Pixel Diving

Building the ultimate mobile diving game

Pixel Diving's Development

I have always loved mobile diving games, but I could never find one that had everything that I wanted to have as a high level diver. This game is going to be my attempt at making the best diving game that I am capable of making. I was a collegiate diver at Indiana University - Purdue University (IUPUI), setting a school record my senior year, and currently coach for Glenbrook Aquatics, one of the most competitive clubs in the Chicagoland area. I have gotten so much from the sport of diving, and creating this game is one of the ways I would like to give back to the diving community.

Current Progress

As this is a passion project, I have postponed its release a few times to ensure that it releases in a state I can be proud of. I have recently migrated Pixel Diving over from Godot to the Unity game engine, but I wasn’t satisfied with just transferring the project in its previous state. The previous art style seemed too simple for where my skills are currently, so I’ve been moving the game from 2D to 3D. This will also let me play more with camera angles and unique perspectives that a traditional 2D game would not allow.
